Below, you can find a Sierpiński triangle. The image was generated by recursively transforming triangles to shift them to places above, to the left, and to the right of the base triangle and then resizing them to be 50% of the previous triangle size. I used the Sierpiński triangle Wikipedia page to help me learn more about the Sierpiński triangle and how to generate it. The controls below allow you to change the depth of the Sierpiński triangle.
