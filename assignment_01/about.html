Biographical Sketch

Before coming to Amherst, I grew up in Philadelphia, PA. Here is a brief description of my life before enrolling in Amherst College: I was born and raised just outside of Philadelphia in Jenkintown, PA. I went to William Penn Charter School, where I graduated in June of 2019. I applied early decision to Amherst in the fall of 2018, and was very excited to be accepted that December! I was always interested in computers, and fell in love with Computer Science when I got to Amherst.

I will be graduating Amherst College in May of 2023. Upon graduation, I will be moving to Denver, CO to return as a software engineer at Sierra Space (where I interned over the Summer of 2022). Last summer, I had to opportunity to work on the Flight Software team for the Dreamchaser 100 spaceplane that will be taking its inaugural flight sometime in 2023. I had an amazing experience and was able to get valuable hands-on work as a software engineer. I am extremely passionate about the aerospace industry and I am excited to be able to work at a company where I get to explore my passions. In addition, I am excited to move out West to Denver and begin a new chapter of my life!
